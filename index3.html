Still lifes

Two months ago I've decided to challenge myself to photograph a couple of still lifes during the week.
Still lifes were originally a popular form of painting that usually depicted household objects, showcasing a slice of life. Photographic still lifes are a new experience of this art form, allowing photographers to create smth interesting out of our usual environment. It's difficult to remain creative and create a good work in a stressful time. But at the same time you don't need to leave the house, you have enough time to take great photos.
