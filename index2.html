Safety orange

Safety orange (or blaze orange) is a hue. Safety orange is used to set objects apart from their surroundings, particularly in complementary contrast to the azure color of the sky. It's characterizes the maturity of personality, energy, power and adrenaline.

One of my associations with this color was adrenaline, because it is the color of energy and hot coals. A girl who look like a superstar from 80's (disco), very daring. She taste a hot red pepper, which symbolizes her power, because not everyone will dare to do it. Also warns that this can be quite dangerous.
